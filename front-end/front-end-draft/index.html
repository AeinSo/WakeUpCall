Your Fatigue Might Be More Than Just 'Puyat'

An estimated 1 in 10 Filipinos unknowingly battles sleep apnea (Philippine Society of Sleep Medicine, 2022)—a dangerous condition that causes breathing during sleep. Left untreated, it silently damages your heart, brain, and daily productivity. But here's hope: diagnosis is simple, and treatment can transform your life.
